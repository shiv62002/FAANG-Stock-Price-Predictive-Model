## Overview

- *How have these companies evolved financially?*  
- *What factors influenced historical price movements?*  
- *Can past performance guide future predictions?*  

To explore this, we analyzed **2013‚Äì2024 financial and stock market data** and developed a model to interpret historical price changes and project future stock performance.
